Privacy Policy

Our Privacy Commitment

Tool-ify is designed with your privacy as a priority. Our tools operate entirely within your browser, and we do not collect, store, or process any of your personal data or the content you input into our tools.

Overview

Welcome to Tool-ify. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection and use of information when you visit our website or use our online tools.

Information We Don't Collect

Our commitment to privacy means we do not collect:

  • Your Input Data: Any text, images, or files you input into our tools remain entirely in your browser and are never sent to our servers.
  • Personal Information: We don't require account creation or any personal details to use our tools.
  • Browsing History: We don't track your browsing patterns across other websites.

Client-Side Processing

All of our tools operate entirely on the client-side, meaning:

  • All processing happens locally in your browser
  • Your data never leaves your device
  • You can even use our tools offline once the page has loaded

Local Storage

Some of our tools may use your browser's local storage capabilities to enhance your experience:

  • Saving your preferences (like dark/light mode)
  • Remembering recent conversions or settings
  • Maintaining tool state between sessions

This information is stored only on your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data that helps us improve our tools. This includes:

  • Anonymous information about which tools are being used
  • General technical information like browser type and device type
  • General location data (country level only)

This data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify individual users. If you prefer, you can use browser extensions or settings to block Google Analytics.

Third-Party Libraries

Our tools may use third-party JavaScript libraries (like utility libraries for formatting, conversion, etc.) that are loaded from Content Delivery Networks (CDNs). These libraries follow their own privacy policies, but they are only used to provide functionality within your browser and do not transmit your data elsewhere.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
